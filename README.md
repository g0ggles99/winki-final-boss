# Surf Points

A small personal activity tracker for building habits that support your surfing.

Open `index.html` in any modern browser. There is nothing to install.

Your activity log and goals are stored privately in that browser's local storage. Use **Manage activities** to adjust the default scoring system or add your own activities.

## Publish free with Netlify

1. Go to [Netlify Drop](https://app.netlify.com/drop) on a computer.
2. Drag this entire `surf-points-tracker` folder onto the page. Keep its contents together—the `assets` folder is needed for the character.
3. Netlify will give you a public web address. Open it on your phone.
4. On iPhone, tap Share, then **Add to Home Screen**. On Android Chrome, tap the menu, then **Install app** or **Add to Home screen**.

The tracker is single-player. Your point history stays in the browser on each device, so the phone version begins as a fresh profile and does not sync with your computer.

Use **Backup progress** in the app to save your entries and goals to a small `.json` file. Keep that file somewhere safe (for example, Files, iCloud Drive, or Google Drive). Use **Restore backup** on a new or reset device to load it again.
